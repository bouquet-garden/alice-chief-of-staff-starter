# Founder ROI Scorecard

This scorecard helps users evaluate whether Alice is actually valuable.

It is intentionally practical.
The point is not to invent vanity metrics. The point is to measure reduction in cognitive load and operational drift.

---

## Core ROI questions

### 1) Did Alice reduce dropped balls?
Track:
- stale commitment count
- unowned tasks discovered
- unresolved decision threads surfaced
- follow-up recovery actions prepared

### 2) Did Alice save founder time?
Track:
- brief generation time saved
- draft preparation time saved
- meeting prep time saved
- reconciliation time saved

### 3) Did Alice improve trust and clarity?
Track:
- correction rate
- false positive rate
- number of useful interventions per week
- how often founder accepts the recommendation unchanged

### 4) Did Alice compound?
Track:
- new reusable rules extracted from incidents
- playbooks created from repeated workflows
- settings improvements after calibration
- memory/world-state coverage growth

---

## Recommended success thresholds

### Week 1
- at least 1 clear magic-moment output
- at least 1 real recovery of dropped work
- correction rate acceptable enough that trust increases, not decreases

### Week 2-3
- visible reduction in stale commitments
- more accepted drafts/briefs with fewer edits
- stable settings profile emerging

### Month 1+
- recurring workflows become normalized
- Alice interventions become expected, not surprising
- founder relies on Alice for operating clarity, not just ad hoc answers

---

## Good vs bad ROI signals

### Good
- "I would have missed that"
- "This saved me 20 minutes"
- "The brief is correct and actionable"
- "I trust the escalation behavior"

### Bad
- "This feels generic"
- "I still have to reconstruct context myself"
- "I don’t know what it can actually do"
- "It keeps interrupting with low-value alerts"

---

## How contributors should use this

Every feature proposal should answer:
1. Which ROI bucket does this improve?
2. What concrete metric should move?
3. How will the user notice the difference in daily operations?
4. What evidence can we link to in the repo?
