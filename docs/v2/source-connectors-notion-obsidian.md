# Source Connectors — Notion + Obsidian

## Notion starter

Core DBs:
- Projects
- Tasks
- Meetings
- People/Accounts
- Experiments
- OKRs

Required properties:
- status, owner, due, priority, estimate, stage
- linked project/entity
- source system
- confidence/review status

## Obsidian PARA starter

- `00 Inbox`
- `01 Projects`
- `02 Areas/Alice Executive Assistant`
- `03 Resources`
- `04 Archive`

## Bridge principle

- Notion = execution truth
- Obsidian = strategic/intellectual truth

Alice should reconcile and cross-link, not force one tool to do both jobs.
