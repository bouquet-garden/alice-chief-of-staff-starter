# First Magic Moment Options

Choose one workflow that is:
- high pain
- low setup
- visibly useful
- grounded in live data
- likely to compound

## Recommended order
1. Meeting prep brief
2. Email ownership / stale-thread brief
3. Executive daily briefing
4. Active deals / follow-up brief
5. Sprint health summary

## Selection rule
Pick the workflow that most quickly makes the founder think:
- “it understands my world”
- “it saved me time already”
- “this is going to get better quickly”
