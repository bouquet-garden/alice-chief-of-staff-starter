# Founder Discovery (Lean Version)

Use these questions early. Don’t over-interview.

1. What do you do, and what does the business look like right now?
2. What are the top 3 things that matter this week?
3. What tends to fall through the cracks?
4. Which systems do you actually live in: email, Notion, Slack, calendar, Obsidian, CRM, GitHub?
5. What do you want Alice to own vs just help with?
6. What would make the first hour already worth it?
