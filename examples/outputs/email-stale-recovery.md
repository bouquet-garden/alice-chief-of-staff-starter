## How stale recovery works

1. **Detection:** Alice scans all active threads nightly. A thread is
   "stale" when ball-with-us and no outbound message exists within the
   configured TTL (default: 7 days).
2. **Commitment matching:** Alice identifies explicit commitments
   ("I'll", "will do", "by EOW") using pattern matching against the
   thread history and cross-references with calendar and task data.
3. **Risk scoring:** Each stale thread is scored on revenue impact,
   relationship tier, days overdue, and whether a specific commitment
   was broken. This determines the priority order.
4. **Draft generation:** Alice generates a recovery reply tailored to
   the situation -- apology for delays, substantive response to the
   original request, and a clear next step.
5. **Approval gate:** No email is sent without Maya's explicit approval.
   She can approve, edit, skip, or reject each draft independently.
