## What this means

The backtest is not a sales pitch. It is a calibration tool. It shows Maya
exactly what Alice would catch, what she would miss, and where the
confidence boundaries are. After reviewing the backtest, Maya can:

1. **Adjust thresholds** -- if 7-day stale detection is too aggressive or
   too lenient, she changes it in the operating contract.
2. **Add blind spots** -- if Alice missed something Maya knows about, she
   can add sources or adjust the signal weights.
3. **Build trust incrementally** -- the backtest proves Alice's judgment
   before she starts acting on live data.

This is the foundation of the trust loop: observe, verify, calibrate, then
gradually expand autonomy.
