# Backtest Engine (v2)

Backtest answers: "If Alice had been live for the last 30/60/90 days, what would she have surfaced?"

## Inputs

- inbox history
- Slack channel/thread history
- Notion project/task history
- meeting transcript history

## Simulation outputs

- stale commitments detected
- unresolved decision threads
- likely missed follow-ups
- contradiction count (cross-surface mismatches)
- potential founder-time saved

## Reporting

Provide a concise scorecard:
- missed-thread count
- stale-commitment count
- estimated high-value interventions
- confidence level per finding

## Why this matters

Backtesting creates immediate trust and ROI legibility for founders.
